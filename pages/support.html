Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions

We use a multi-step verification process including business registration checks, compliance audits, and reference verification. All suppliers must provide current certifications and undergo our quality assessment before joining the platform.

FoodXchange supports major international standards including FDA, EU regulations, kosher certification, organic standards, halal requirements, and country-specific import regulations. Our automated compliance checker ensures all requirements are met.

With FoodXchange, sourcing projects typically complete in 6-12 weeks compared to 6+ months with traditional methods. The exact timeline depends on product complexity, compliance requirements, and sample testing needs.

We accept major credit cards, bank transfers, and ACH payments for subscription fees. For transactions between buyers and suppliers, we support various payment methods including letters of credit, wire transfers, and escrow services.

Yes! We offer API integrations for ERP systems, inventory management, and logistics platforms. Our technical team can help you set up custom integrations to streamline your workflow.

Our support team provides end-to-end assistance for any issues. We help mediate disputes, coordinate with logistics providers, and work with suppliers to resolve problems quickly. All transactions have built-in protection mechanisms.
