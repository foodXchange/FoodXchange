The Problem We Solve

Global food sourcing is broken. A single specification error—like the wrong cornflake color—can lead to complete product recalls, wasted months of work, and massive financial losses.

Fragmented Communication

Emails, spreadsheets, and WhatsApp messages cause lost details, delays, and confusion across teams.

Costly Delays

Disconnected teams working in silos lead to information gaps and supply chain failures.

Expensive Mistakes

Critical compliance errors compound throughout the supply chain, leading to recalls and lost revenue.

Real Impact: The Cornflake Incident

One minor specification error—wrong cornflake color—caused a full product recall. This single mistake collapsed an entire 9-month sourcing project, resulting in massive time, money, and opportunity losses.

Meet Our Founder

Udi Strykowski founded FoodXchange after witnessing firsthand how broken processes in global food sourcing lead to massive losses and missed opportunities.

With years of experience in the food industry, Udi saw how small specification errors could derail entire projects, how fragmented communication created costly delays, and how compliance challenges prevented businesses from scaling globally.

His vision for FoodXchange is simple: create a unified platform that transforms chaotic, error-prone sourcing processes into structured, reliable workflows that empower businesses of all sizes to compete globally.

"Every business deserves access to enterprise-level sourcing tools. Small importers should be able to compete with global giants, and no one should lose months of work due to a simple specification error."

Udi Strykowski

Our Mission
